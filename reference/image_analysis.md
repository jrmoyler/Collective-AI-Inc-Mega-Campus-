## Architectural Design Language Summary

### Visual Identity
- **Futuristic mega-campus** with coherent planning language
- **Hexagonal cell grid** unifies the site
- **Blue-green glowing borders** on roads/paths (kinetic tile/smart infrastructure aesthetic)
- **Dense mature landscaping** softens industrial and data zones
- **Water as central feature** — rivers, ponds, reflective pools
- **Solar infrastructure visible** — not hidden, part of campus identity
- **Night-readable** — buildings glow, roads illuminate

### Material Palette
- Dark charcoal/near-black metal: data/utility buildings
- Light stone/concrete: civic/governance buildings
- White metal/glass: research/lab buildings
- Warm terracotta/earth: residential/hotel
- Bright green/glass: farm/life-science
- Industrial corrugated: manufacturing/logistics

### Roof Forms
- Flat with equipment (data centers)
- Sawtooth monitor (industrial)
- Barrel vault/curved (civic/library)
- Dome (observatory, civic core)
- Crystalline/stepped (HQ tower)
- Green living (residential, research)
- Glass greenhouse (farm, bio-energy)
- Arched canopy (transport)

### Special Site Features
- Solar panels integrated into parking canopies and rooftops
- Algae ponds as visible landscape element (bright green)
- Wind turbines at campus perimeter  
- Kinetic paving on main promenades
- Blue-green stormwater swales running E-W
- Pedestrian bridges over road network
